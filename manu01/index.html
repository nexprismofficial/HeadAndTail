How to Play (Rule)

The strategy of the game is as follows: You always bet only on one of any sides. Let's take the Heads side for example. If you win, you must bet the minimum bet. If you lose, double your bet until you win. After you win, you need to return the bet again. And change the coin to "Tail". With this approach, in any case, you will be in the black. In fact, everything is quite simple.

Please Read Following Instructions Carefully before playing game:

  1. In this Timer Starts from 150 Seconds during this you can select or modify Head or Tail option and edit bet amount this can only apply in between 150 seconds to 10 seconds.
  2. When Time reaches 10 seconds you can't modify any bet amount and Head or Tail Option whatever changes you want you can only do in between 150 seconds to 10 seconds Only.
  3. When Timer Reaches 0 seconds the coin will flip automatically and display the results.
  4. If you enter amount but not select any option you will lose the game
